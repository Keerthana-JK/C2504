19. To find average of two numbers

Pseudo:
		input num1, num2
		sum = num1+num2
		avg = sum/2
		print avg

Pseudo routine:
		function FindAvgof2(num1, num2)
		sum = num1+num2
		avg = sum/2
		return avg

Pseudo test:
		procedure TestFindAvgof2()
		input num1, num2
		avg=FindAvgof2(num1,num2)
		print(avg)
